CS Decibel Meter Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

CS Decibel Meter ("we," "our," or "this app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

CS Decibel Meter is a simple decibel measurement tool. We commit to:

  • No Personal Information Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, phone number, etc.).
  • Audio Data: This app requires access to your device's microphone to measure ambient sound levels. All audio processing is done locally on your device, and we do not record, store, or transmit any audio data.
  • No Data Collection: This app does not include any analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party data collection services.

3. Permission Usage

This app requires the following permission:

  • Microphone Permission: Used to measure ambient sound levels in real-time. Audio data is processed only locally on your device and is not saved to device storage or transmitted to any servers.

4. Data Usage

The only purposes for which we use microphone permission are:

  • To calculate ambient sound levels (decibel values) in real-time
  • To display current, maximum, minimum, average, and other statistical data in the app interface

All calculations are performed on your device, and data never leaves your device.

5. Data Storage

This app does not store any audio data or measurement records on your device. All statistical data is only kept in memory while the app is running and is cleared when the app is closed.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties because we do not collect any data.

7. Third-Party Services

This app does not include any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection tools.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will immediately delete such information.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, you do not need to worry about data breaches or requesting data deletion. You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device settings.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
