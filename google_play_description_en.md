#### Core Features

**Real-Time Measurement**
- One-tap start, instantly displays current ambient sound decibel levels with simple, intuitive operation
- High-precision audio sampling for accurate sound intensity reflection
- Intuitive progress bar display with color-coded noise level indicators

**Statistical Analysis**
- Real-time display of minimum, maximum, and average values
- Percentile statistics (P50, P90, P95) to help you understand noise distribution
- Peak recording feature to capture the highest noise moments

- **Home Environment**: Monitor household noise to protect hearing health

#### Privacy Protection

We value your privacy. The app only accesses microphone permissions when you actively start measurement and does not collect any audio data in the background. All measurement data is stored only on your device and is not uploaded to any server.
